steps
    Game of Life Sequencer

    This is a step sequencer that uses the algorithm of John Conway's Game of Life to generate evolving patterns.

    The top four rows are a sawtooth synth, the middle four are drum samples, and the bottom four are a sine synth. Clicking on cells will toggle their state.

    Patterns may seem random at first, but longer evolve intervals (16 and above) reveal gradual shifts in rhythm and melody.

    Made by Gilad Mayani using Tone.js, Tone.js UI, and Tone.js audio samples. Source code on GitHub.
